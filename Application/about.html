Having an experience of 5 years in this industry, we have certified professionals artists who believe that every woman is beautiful and some good makeup adds glamour to her personality. We understand the skin type of the client and apply makeup accordingly. We blend the makeup perfectly to suit the different skin tones. We only use high-end products such as M.A.C, Bobbi Brown, Urbandecay, Kryolan, etc. With our creativity and intense skills, we create magic on the face enhancing her features naturally and giving her a stunning look. We are one of the most reasonable makeup artists in Pune to get your makeup done for your pre-wedding, wedding, photoshoots or any other functions.

We provide training with different packages including makeups, dressing and hairstyles. We have a fully equipped studio. Every student gets personal attention because we allow a limited number of candidates in a batch. Every candidate gets hands-on experience with models and photoshoots. Trainings are conducted based on time suitable for every candidate in a batch. Our state of the art studio and high-end makeup products make it easy for every student to get familiar with the current trends of the industry.
